About

Welcome to my photography portfolio website! My name is Justin and I am a student photographer with 5 years of experience based in San Bernadino County.

Over the past 5 years, I have honed my skills through countless hours of practice and experimentation. I am constantly seeking out new techniques and approaches to photography, and I am passionate about using my skills to bring out the best in every subject I photograph.

When I am not behind the lens, you can often find me exploring new places, seeking out inspiration in the world around me, or working on my latest creative project. My passion for photography is truly a part of who I am, and I am excited to share my work with you on this website.

Thank you for taking the time to explore my portfolio. I hope my images inspire you as much as they have inspired me. If you are interested in working together or have any questions, please don't hesitate to get in touch. I would love to hear from you!
